WHAT WE DO

PeerPods helps you explore careers before choosing them.

Most students pick careers based on what their parents suggest or what their friends are doing. Very few actually know what the job looks like from the inside.

PeerPods connects you with real professionals in your domain of interest. You talk to them, ask real questions, and complete a mini simulation of their actual work — so you can decide with confidence.

Career Conversations

Talk directly with a professional currently working in your field of interest. Ask anything. Get honest answers.

Job Simulations

Complete a short real-world task from that profession. Experience the work before committing to it.

Career Clarity

Leave with a clear understanding of whether the career is right for you — and what your next steps should be.

HOW IT WORKS

Experience Before You Decide

Choosing a career shouldn't be based on guesswork. PeerPods helps students explore careers through a structured 7-day experience that combines learning, practice, and professional interaction.

1

Learn the Basics

Build a foundational understanding of your chosen career — the core skills, tools, responsibilities, and industry knowledge required for the role.

2

Experience a Job Simulation

Apply what you've learned through a hands-on simulation designed to replicate real-world tasks and challenges faced by professionals in the field.

3

Discover Your Fit
