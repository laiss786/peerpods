WHAT IS PEERPODS

Pods: courses you can try before you commit to them.

Most students commit years — and lakhs of rupees — to a field they've never actually experienced. PeerPods flips that. We break trending, in-demand courses into small "pods": short, structured experiences you can complete in days, not years.

Every pod teaches you the basics, drops you into a hands-on simulation of the real work, and — if you like what you feel — connects you with a mentor already working in that field, before you spend serious time or money going deeper.

Micro Courses

Bite-sized modules covering exactly what a beginner needs to know about a trending field — no fluff, no semester-long syllabus.

Real Simulations

Every pod ends with a task modeled on real work in that field, so you feel the job — not just read about it.

Mentor Access

Curious enough to go further? Get connected with a working professional in that domain before committing bigger time or money.
